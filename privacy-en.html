1. Data controller

Fan Xuyang, Individual Developer · 7@notescience.com · Family Cash Flow (budgeting app with cloud sync and optional family sharing).

2. What we collect

Account data (Apple ID, optional name/email, display ID); financial and household content you enter; device/technical logs for sign-in and sync. No ad/analytics SDKs in the current App. Optional App lock stays on your device—we do not receive biometrics.

3. How we use data

To run the App, sync devices, enable family collaboration, secure accounts, fix issues, and comply with law. We do not sell ledger data or use it for third-party ads.

4. Security

  • HTTPS in transit;
  • Encryption at rest on our cloud host;
  • Sign in with Apple + row-level security for account/household isolation;
  • Operator access only when needed for support, security, or legal compliance—not routine browsing;
  • Processors: Apple (auth), Supabase (database/sync).

5. Can the developer see my data?

Direct answer: With cloud sync, your ledger is stored on our cloud database. As the operator, we technically can access data through controlled admin tools when necessary (fixes, security, support you request, legal compliance)—unlike a phone-only app where the developer has no access.

We do not proactively read your transactions in normal operations. We do not sell your ledger or use it for ads. We use HTTPS, encryption at rest, and access controls.

Note: If you use family sharing, members you invite can see that household ledger—that is your choice, separate from developer access.

6. International transfers & retention

Data may be stored/processed outside your country. We retain data while you use the App; delete or anonymize after account deletion except where law requires retention.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location: access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability where applicable, withdraw consent, complain to a supervisor. Contact 7@notescience.com (subject: Family Cash Flow - Privacy).

8. Children & law

Not directed at children under 16. Governed by PRC law; mandatory local consumer rights still apply where required. See also Terms of Service.
