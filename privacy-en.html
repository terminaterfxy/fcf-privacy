1. Introduction

Welcome to Family Cash Flow (Chinese product name: 家庭现金流; “the App” or “we”).

The App is provided by Fan Xuyang, Individual Developer (“Operator” or “we”) for personal and household expense tracking, budgets, asset views, and collaborative family bookkeeping. The developer name shown on the App Store follows your Apple Developer account registration.

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, how we store and protect it, who we share it with, and your rights. By signing in or continuing to use the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

Important: The App does not offer end-to-end encryption (E2EE). Your ledger data is encrypted in transit and stored encrypted in the cloud for multi-device sync and family collaboration. To operate the service, keep it secure, and comply with law, the Operator and our cloud service providers may access and process this data—similar to mainstream budgeting apps.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account and identity

  • Sign in with Apple: Production builds support Apple sign-in only. We may receive identifiers from Apple; on first authorization you may optionally share your name and email (Apple may provide these only once).
  • In-app profile: Nickname/username, system-assigned display ID, language and UI preferences.
  • We do not require a separate App password in production.

2.2 Financial and household data you provide

  • Categories, amounts, notes, period status;
  • Asset/contract-style entries;
  • Household membership, invites, join/leave records;
  • Currency, projection rate, budget, and dashboard settings.

This is sensitive financial information. Only invite people you trust.

2.3 Device and logs

  • Device type, OS version, App version;
  • Network connectivity;
  • Essential logs for sign-in, sync, and troubleshooting.

Current production build: We do not use separate third-party advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs.

2.4 Data on your device

Some non-core data may be stored locally (e.g. last viewed period, onboarding flags). Main synced data can be restored after you sign in again.

2.5 Optional features (if enabled)

App lock (Face ID / Touch ID / device passcode or in-app PIN): Used only on your device. We do not upload biometric data to our servers.

4. Storage and Security

  • Data is mainly stored in Supabase (infrastructure may be outside mainland China).
  • HTTPS protects data in transit; encryption at rest is provided by our hosting stack.
  • Sign-in and row-level security (RLS) limit access to your account and authorized family data.
  • Household members can view and edit shared family ledger data.
  • The App is not end-to-end encrypted; the Operator and processors may access data for operations, support, security, and legal compliance.
  • After account deletion, we delete or anonymize your data except where law requires retention.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

Shared family ledger data is visible to members of that family. We use Apple, Supabase, and related cloud infrastructure to provide the App. We do not share your ledger for third-party marketing without consent, except as required by law or to protect users.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, delete records, delete your account, withdraw consent, and complain to us or authorities. Family admins with remaining members may need to transfer admin or remove members before deletion. We respond within 15 business days where possible.

7. Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. Contact us to request deletion if you are a guardian.

8. Changes

We may update this policy and notify you in the App for material changes. Continued use means acceptance.
